About Us

Wildlife rescue is a nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglect, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescue is a member of the National Wildlife Rehbilitators Association

Visit www.nwrawildlife.org

We help many animals, such as:

  • Racoon
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles
